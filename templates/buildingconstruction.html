Department of Building and Civil Engineering

Head of Department - Cheror Felix

Trainees from the department can be employed in ever growing construction industry, and civil Engineering industry. Potential employers include real estate development companies, building and construction companies, ministry of works, Kenya National Highways Authority, Standard Gauge Railway Project. Graduates can also pursue careers in research institutions or further their studies in building and civil engineering in higher institutions. The following are courses in the department;

  • Diploma in Building Technology
  • Certificate in Building Technology (Masonry)
  • Certificate in Plumbing
  • Artisan in Masonry
  • Artisan plumbing & Pipe fitting
