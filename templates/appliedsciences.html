DEPARTMENT OF APPLIED SCIENCES

HEAD OF DEPARTMENT: MRS MUTAI GETRUDE

The department is geared to equip trainees in the field of Applied science and agricultural value addition courses, with the right knowledge and skills so as to have and develop an understanding of agriculture and its importance to the family and the nation, promote interest in agriculture as an industry and create awareness of opportunities existing in agriculture and related sectors, enhance skills needed in carrying out agricultural practices and promote agricultural activities which enhance environmental conservation.

These are courses under the department;

  • Diploma in Food science & Processing Technology
  • Diploma in General Agriculture
  • Diploma in Entrepreneurial Agriculture
  • Diploma in Analytical Chemistry
  • Diploma in Applied Biology
  • Food science and processing technology
  • Certificate in General Agriculture
  • Certificate in Science Laboratory Technology
  • Certificate in Food and Beverage
  • Artisan Food and Beverage
