ABOUT US

Kiptaragon TVC is a middle level college which was established and started in the year 2019 and owned by the government of Kenya. It is located in a serene environment 2.5km off the highway Molo-Olenguruone-Bomet road.

The institution stands on an 18ha piece of land and adjasent to Kiptaragon Primary School. Currently Kiptaragon tVC offers Diploma, Certificate and Artisan courses in Business, ICT & Computer Studies, Applied Sciences and Building & Construction. The institution is also equiped with ultra modern facilities and equipments for processing as well as packaging animal products - milk and meat.
