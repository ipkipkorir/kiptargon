ABOUT US

Kiptaragon Technical & Vocational College is a government institution which was established under the act of parliament in the Ministry of Education, Science & Technology - State Department of Technical Vocational Education Training TVET. It had its first intake in September 2019 and now we have student population of about 300.

The institution offer courses in Artisan, Certificate & Diploma in two main Departments namely;

  • Technical Department with two sections
    1. Applied Science
    2. Building & Civil Engineering
  • Business Department with two sections
    1. ICT
    2. Business

The institution is governed with the guidance of TVET policies and its Vision, Mission and Core values as outlined below and embedded with Christian virtues.

Vision

To be a center of excellence in Technical and Vocational Education Training

Mission

To promote and impact Vocational Competencies and entrepreneurial skills for socio-economic self-reliance

Core Values

  • Integrity
  • Team work
  • Transparency & Accountability
  • Innovation & Creativity
  • Health & Safety
  • Quality Services
